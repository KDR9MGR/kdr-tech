Meta ads strategy (under $500/mo to start)
Month 1–3: Retargeting only
  • Budget: $200–300/mo
  • Audience: website visitors + video viewers
  • Ad: client feedback video → book a call CTA
  • Objective: conversions (Calendly bookings)
Month 4–6: Prospecting added
  • Budget: $400–500/mo
  • Audience: US/UK/AU founders, startup owners
  • Ad: case study carousel → lead magnet
  • Lookalike from website visitors
Winning ad formats for you
  • Raw client video testimonials (no editing)
  • Before/after app demo screencast
  • "We built this in 4 weeks" speed build
  • Mini vlog clip → book a call CTA
What to never do
  • Don't run ads before you have proof (testimonials)
  • Don't use stock photos or generic creatives
  • Don't target India — target US/UK/AU only
  • Don't boost posts — use Ads Manager
